Hosting

Digital Ocean: I previously hosted mail, web, and XMPP on a Digital Ocean VM. It was smooth sailing.

Chunkhost: Before Digital Ocean I used a Xen-based VM from Chunkhost. No problems there either.

Shopify: I've built two e-commerce sites with Shopify, and would do so again.
